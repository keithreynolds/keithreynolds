Frank Reynolds

2/26/1943 - 12/11/2025

Frank is survived by his children Keith and Sofia, his daughter-in-law, Mary and five grandchildren: William, Henry, Chloe, Tessa & Seth.

Frank was born in Los Angeles, California on February 26th, 1943 to Robert Reynolds & Enid Burdick. He had one older brother, Robert and is survived by his two younger sisters, Kathy & Patti. After graduating from Glendale High School, Frank briefly worked as a postman. Frank met his beloved wife Giovanna in Verona while serving in the US Army Southern European Task Force. Frank proposed to Giovanna on her birthday and they married three months later. Together, they moved to Glendale, CA and in 1973 welcomed their first child, Keith. Five years later they welcomed a daughter, Sofia and brought her home to Huntington Beach where they lived for 45 years.

Those years were spent working, raising their two children, traveling in Europe to visit family, walking the beach hand-in-hand and enjoying time with lifelong friends.
