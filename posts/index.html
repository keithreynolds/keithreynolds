Product Manager, Private Pilot

In a time a never-ending newsletters, I decided to archive some of my favorite posts here.

I like flying airplanes, and I like product management. Sometimes I wrote about one, or the other. And sometimes I write about both at the same time.

I took this photo while departing Santa Barbara's iconic airport KSBA on my first 150nm solo xc. Just under the wing is UC Santa Barbara. I remember a great satisfaction, fulfilling a vision I had at 18, watching departing GA traffic from an ocean view dorm at the Anacapa.

My journey to product management (and General Aviation!) was not typical, but they each have a lot in common.
